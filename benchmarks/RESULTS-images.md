## Method

- Fixed prompt: "a weathered lighthouse on a rocky cliff at golden hour, dramatic clouds, photorealistic, highly detailed".
- Per checkpoint: 1 discarded warm-up (cold checkpoint load), then 3 measured generations; table shows the **median**.
- Steps/CFG are each checkpoint's own commonly published defaults, not a
  single pinned setting — SDXL, FLUX.1-schnell and FLUX.1-dev are meant to
  be run very differently, so forcing identical settings would benchmark
  an artificial scenario rather than real usage. FLUX uses guidance
  distillation (a `FluxGuidance` node) instead of classic CFG, which is
  why its CFG is pinned to 1.0.
- VRAM/RAM peaks are sampled every 0.5 s during generation; models are
  explicitly unloaded (`POST /free`) between checkpoints so cold-load and
  peak-memory numbers aren't polluted by a previous model still resident.
