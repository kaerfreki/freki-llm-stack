MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT (excerpt)

1. Parties. This Agreement is entered into between Vextron Analytics SAS,
registered with the RCS of Lyon under no. 512 384 776 (the "Provider"), and
Halden Logistics GmbH, Hamburg (the "Client").

2. Term. This Agreement takes effect on 15 March 2026 (the "Effective
Date") for an initial term of twenty-four (24) months. It renews
automatically for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party
gives written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days before the
end of the then-current term.

3. Fees and Payment. The Client shall pay the Provider a fixed fee of
EUR 18,500 per month, invoiced monthly in advance. Invoices are payable
within forty-five (45) days of the invoice date. Fees are indexed by 3% at
each renewal.

4. Termination. (a) The Client may terminate this Agreement for
convenience at any time after the twelfth (12th) month of the initial
term, subject to sixty (60) days' prior written notice. (b) Either party
may terminate for material breach if the breaching party fails to cure
within thirty (30) days of written notice of the breach.

5. Liability. Each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement is
capped at the total fees paid by the Client in the twelve (12) months
preceding the event giving rise to the claim. This cap does not apply to
breaches of Section 6 (Confidentiality) or Section 7 (Intellectual
Property).

6. Confidentiality. Each party shall protect the other party's
confidential information with at least the care it applies to its own.

7. Intellectual Property. All pre-existing intellectual property remains
the property of its owner; deliverables are licensed, not assigned.

8. Data Protection. All Client data shall be hosted in data centres
located within the European Union and shall not be transferred outside the
EEA without the Client's prior written consent.

9. Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by French law. The parties
submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Tribunal de commerce de Lyon.
